While researching UPI fraud, I realized how easily attackers trick users using urgency. Many people think approving a payment request means receiving money, but it actually sends money. This surprised me the most. I also learned that sharing OTP or scanning unknown QR codes can lead to financial loss. I will change my habit of responding quickly to unknown calls. I will verify all payment requests carefully and educate my friends about UPI fraud. This awareness will help me stay safe online.

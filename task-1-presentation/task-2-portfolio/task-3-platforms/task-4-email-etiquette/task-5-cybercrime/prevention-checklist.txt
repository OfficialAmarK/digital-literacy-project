Stay Safe Online Checklist

1. Never approve unknown UPI collect requests
2. Do not share OTP, PIN, or passwords
3. Verify caller identity before acting
4. Avoid installing unknown apps
5. Use strong passwords
6. Enable two-factor authentication
7. Do not scan unknown QR codes
8. Monitor bank transactions regularly
9. Avoid clicking suspicious links
10. Keep UPI apps updated

UPI Safety Tips:
- Always verify receiver before sending money
- Reject collect requests from unknown users

Report Cybercrime:
National Cyber Crime Portal: cybercrime.gov.in
